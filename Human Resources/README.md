# 👥 Human Resources Workflows

A collection of Human Resources (HR) automation workflows built with n8n, focused on streamlining employee lifecycle management, identity administration, onboarding, offboarding, compliance, and internal HR operations.

This folder serves as a growing library of automation patterns designed to reduce manual administrative work, improve operational efficiency, and ensure consistent employee management processes across various HR systems and business applications.

---

## What You'll Find

Workflows in this category may include:

- 👤 Employee onboarding
- 🚪 Employee offboarding
- 🔑 User account provisioning
- 🛡️ Identity & access management
- 📄 HR document processing
- 📝 Employee record management
- 🎓 Training & certification tracking
- 📅 Leave and attendance automation
- 📢 Employee notifications
- 💼 Recruitment support
- 🔍 Compliance and audit workflows
- 📊 HR reporting

As the repository grows, additional Human Resources automation scenarios will be added.
