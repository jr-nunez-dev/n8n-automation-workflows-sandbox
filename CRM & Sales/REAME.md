# 💼 CRM & Sales Workflows

A collection of CRM and sales automation workflows built with n8n, covering customer lifecycle management, lead processing, data enrichment, routing, synchronization, and sales operations.

This folder serves as a growing library of automation patterns designed to streamline repetitive CRM tasks, improve data quality, and help sales teams operate more efficiently through system integrations and intelligent workflow design.

---

## What You'll Find

Workflows in this category may include:

- 🎯 Lead qualification and scoring
- 🔍 Lead enrichment
- 🌍 Geographic routing
- 🔀 Smart lead assignment
- 🔄 CRM synchronization
- 📊 Sales reporting
- 🤝 Customer onboarding
- 📈 Pipeline automation
- 📨 Sales notifications
- 🏢 Account management

As the repository grows, additional CRM and sales automation scenarios will be added.
