# 🛒 Commerce & Operations Workflows

A collection of commerce and operations automation workflows built with n8n, focused on streamlining order processing, inventory management, fulfillment, procurement, logistics, and day-to-day operational activities.

This folder serves as a growing library of automation patterns designed to improve operational efficiency, reduce manual processing, and enable seamless data flow between business systems throughout the commerce and operations lifecycle.

---

## What You'll Find

Workflows in this category may include:

- 🛍️ Order processing
- 📦 Inventory management
- 🚚 Shipping and fulfillment
- 🏭 Warehouse operations
- 🔄 ERP synchronization
- 📋 Procurement automation
- 📑 Purchase order management
- 💳 Payment processing
- 📦 Supply chain coordination
- 📊 Operational reporting
- 📈 Demand forecasting
- 🔔 Operational notifications

As the repository grows, additional commerce and operations automation scenarios will be added.
